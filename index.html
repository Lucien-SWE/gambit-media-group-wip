Our Story

Gambit Media Group began in August 2025, originally known as Gambit SMP — a Minecraft server dedicated to bringing creators together through collaborative gameplay and content. After ownership transitioned to MayhemForge, our vision expanded beyond a single server.

Our Goal

At Gambit Media Group, our goal is to build a collaborative community where aspiring content creators can grow, inspire, and achieve more together. We believe in the power of teamwork — creators supporting creators — to bring ambitious ideas to life. We host events, video collaborations, and operate a Minecraft server to create chances for creators to showcase and grow.
