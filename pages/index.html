♣ ♦ ♥ ♠ About Us ♣ ♦ ♥ ♠

Our Story

Gambit Media Group began in August 2025, originally known as Gambit SMP — a Minecraft server dedicated to bringing creators together through collaborative gameplay and content. After ownership transitioned to MayhemForge, our vision expanded beyond a single server.

What started as a small Minecraft community evolved into a full media group built around creativity, collaboration, and growth. Today, Gambit Media Group focuses on supporting a diverse community of upcoming content creators across various platforms and genres — united not just by games, but by a shared passion for creating and inspiring others.

♣ ♦ ♥ Our Goal ♦ ♥ ♠

At Gambit Media Group, our goal is to build a collaborative community where aspiring content creators can grow, inspire, and achieve more together. We believe in the power of teamwork — creators supporting creators — to bring ambitious ideas to life.

Join Us

As part of your application, we ask that you create a 30-second to 1-minute video that showcases your personality. Use this time to talk about your hobbies, interests, or personal projects, and let us see what makes you unique. You're encouraged to express your creativity through editing and presentation, but editing isn't required — what matters most is that your video reflects who you are and how you bring your individuality to the community.

Contact Us

We primarily operate through our Discord server, where our community and staff stay active and connected. For inquiries outside Discord, you can also reach us by email or visit the official Gambit Media YouTube channel to see what our members are creating and stay updated on our latest projects.
